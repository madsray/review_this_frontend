ABOUT THIS APP

This movie app is one of a kind. It allows you to review good and bad food pairings with any movie out there. Don't see your movie? Simply click the create movie button at the top and fill out the form. Want to review one of the movies? Click the movie poster and it will lead you to a page where you can read about the movie as well add your suggestion for a dinner pairing. Bon Appetit!
